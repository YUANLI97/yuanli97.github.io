01 Research

In China, the IT companies are now the most competitive strength. Its growth, which is usually compared with American companies, is now a symbol power. In this case, more and more people pay close attention to it and it is becoming a platform for various countries to compete with each other.

Thanks to its rapid development and importance in countries’ power, thousands of posts are created. The positions contain not only high-demand ones, but also simple ones for low-level workers. Identification workers are part of them.

In the future, the development of technology may dramatically reform our life-style, and to prepare ourselves with the unknown changes, we better understand the developing technologies first. Thus, in this project I would like to build an interactive exhibition hall from which people can learn about the A.I. technology and the techniques required.

The identification workers are usually ignored in this industry. Since their job is basically to add labels on images, which may not sound challenging, many people focus more on programmers, engineers and so on. However, their contribution to the IT industry is invaluable. If we don’t have the accurate data they provide, we won’t be able to train those powerful neural network models.

Currently, the base labor workers (identification workers) are less-paid. Their family, education and physical condition are generally worse than engineers as well. They certainly need more care and welfare to live a comfortable life. Thus, I think this design project also needs to emphasize the hard working of the base labor workers.

According to the workflow, personally I would like to emphasize on data obtaining, data identification and final result generation in the exhibition. Visual notations used in the technology principles can be applied in our design, finally reproducing the workflow in this industry.
