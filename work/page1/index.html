00 Design Task设计目标

寻找/定义当前Shopee APP内最重要的UX设计问题。解释你选择它的原因，提供相应的调研资料，并给出你的解决方案。 Find the most critical UX issue from current Shopee APP. Explain the identification process, share your research results and provide your solution for it.

01 Background 项目背景

Introduction 介绍

Shopee is the leading online shopping platform in south-east Asia.

Current major functions:

Potential new functions: Successful new functions can be the factor to change the game. For example, in mainland China, Taobao Live has changed the way people shop and helped Taobao to further expand its market size. Currently, ShopeePay and Shopee Live have revealed Shopee’s ambition to lead the industry. 成功的新功能能成为改变游戏规则的存在。在中国大陆，淘宝直播已改变了人们网上购物的方式，并且帮助淘宝扩大了他们的市场占有率。 目前，ShopeePay和Shopee Live已揭示了Shopee想要领导行业的野心。

Purpose of Redesign 改版目的

South-east Asia Market Southeast Asia is in the very nascent stages of e-commerce development. Thus, to attract users who started using online shopping recently can be important. 东南亚市场还处在电商发展未成熟的阶段。因此，吸引刚加入/准备使用电商平台的新人是当前十分重要的任务。

Business Objectives Ultimate objective: Enlarge market share and GMV (Gross Merchandise Volume) Intermediate objective: promote new functions, attract new users, keep old users, encourage more deal, … **最终目标：**扩大市场占有率和成交总额 **中间目标：**推广新功能，吸引新用户，留存老用户，提升交易量，…

To achieve the ultimate objective requires us to meet intermediate objectives first. By listing intermediate objectives, we can brainstorm many kinds of design directions and ensure that they can have a positive effect on Shopee by research. 我们需要通过逐渐实现中间目标来达成我们的最终目标。通过列出这些中间目标，我们能头脑风暴出许多设计的方向与可能性，并通过调研来最终找到能为Shopee带来收益的优化方式。

Possible Design Directions 各种可行性

Listed are some general improvements that can be done for e-shopping platforms 以下列出了一些线上购物平台常见的优化点

Functional Framework 功能架构

By sorting out Shopee’s functions, we can also find some improvable points: 通过整理Shopee现有的功能，我们能发现其存在的一些可优化的点：

  1. Rough classification

Structures of Shopee can be classified more neatly. Notifications in the tab bar can be replaced by Shopping cart so that users can view shopping cart more easily and then become more likely to purchase products in the shopping cart. Also,** Shopee live** is a powerful social function and can be added to Feed page.

  1. 粗糙的分类

Shopee的各类功能结构可以被分类得更整洁：tab bar的通Notification页面可以考虑替换为Shopping Cart页面，让用户更轻易地查看自己的购物车，进而提升了他们从购物车内结账地意愿。从战略意义的角度考虑，Shopee Live作为一个有潜力的社交功能也可以被添加到Feed页面。

  1. Redundant function

Some functions appeared in multiple times. ‘Coins’ and ‘ShopeePay’ appeared in both Me page and Daily Discover page. ‘Post’ and ‘Live’ appeared in both Daily Discover page and Feed page. ‘Scan’ even appeared twice in Daily Discover page (by using ‘Scan’ or ‘Search’).

  1. 多余、重复的功能

Shopee内有一些出现了多次。‘Coins’ 和 ‘ShopeePay’同时出现在了Me页面和Daily Discovery页面。‘Scan’功能甚至在Daily Discovery这一个页面出现了2次。

  1. Massive information

The homepage has covered too many functions that users may get tired by cluttered design. For example, to reach ‘product recommendation’, users have to go through many contents that are unattractive to them.

  1. 超量的信息

Shopee的首页包含了太多不同的功能，导致用户的兴趣很容易被混乱的设计降低。例如，在到达’Product Recommendation’这一内容前，用户需要浏览很多不具有吸引力的内容。

02 Research 调研

Research Objective 调研目的

These directions can give us designers a good start to brainstorm different aspects of redesign. However, to find the most critical one requires us to conduct a more detailed research. Thus, during later research, I aim to figure out the most influential issue or defect. 以上的设计方向给设计师们的改版工作带来了一个好的开头。然而，要找到当下最重要的设计点要求我们进行一些更细致的调研。因此，我通过接下来的调研来寻找当下最能影响Shopee的设计点。

Qualitative Research 定型调查

User Interview

Although most of my friends are from mainland China, I still managed to find 2 Singaporean friends who use Shopee regularly to explore their personal feelings about online shopping on Shopee. 虽然我大部分的朋友都来自中国大陆，我还是找到了2个新加坡的经常使用Shopee的朋友来分享他们使用时的个人感受。有趣的是，他们两位都认为Shopee的banner并不够吸引人，但我的中国朋友们通常觉得国内在线购物APP的banner是十分有诱惑力的。

Based on existing users’ feedback, we can conclude some major defects:

  • ✅Unattractive Banner: Banners are very important for commercial promotion and they can attract users to click and then more likely to facilitate a deal. We may move banners lower to the eye level instead.
  • ✅Unnecessary Functions: Many functions (like Shopee Live, Shopee Story, ShopeePay) are not commonly used. Balancing the importance of both promoting new functions and modifying user experience, we may eliminate some functions and redesign others into a more attractive design.
  • ❌Higher price than other platforms: Users care a lot about the price and if they find a product cheaper (with same quality and service) on other platforms, Shopee may loss their trust.
  • ❌Limited range of products: Some users claim they can’t find the target product on Shopee.

✅ Solvable only by design ❌ Demand collaboration of multiple departments

根据现有的用户反馈，我们可以总结得出几个主要的产品缺陷：

  • ✅**不吸引人的Banner：**Banner对于商业推广是十分重要的，它们能快速地吸引用户点击内容，并提高成单量。我们可以把banner向下移动，让它跟用户的视线能在同一高度。
  • ✅**非必要的功能：**Shopee的许多功能(如Shopee Live, Shopee Story, ShopeePay)并没有被用户接受/使用。平衡了推广新功能和优化用户体验的重要性之后，我们可以移除部分功能，并把其他的功能设计得更吸引人。
  • ❌**更高的货物价格：**在网购时，用户对价格十分敏感。如果他们发现相同质量/服务的产品在其他平台更便宜，那Shopee可能会逐渐失去用户的信任。
  • ❌**有限的产品种类：**部分用户表示他们在Shopee上找不到自己想要的产品。

✅ 可通过设计解决 ❌ 需要其他各部门合作

Interestingly, they both said that Shopee’s banner is not attractive at all while my Chinese friends think Chinese e-commerce APPs have very tempting banners. Besides, interviewees said Shopee had done a great design job in search function, product intro list, product detail information, after-sales service, return service and so on. In short, they had nice experience during and after they buy a product, and what we can focus to improve now is probably the experience before users decide to buy a product. 有趣的事，他们两位都认为Shopee目前的banner并不够吸引人；而我的中国朋友们都觉得中国的网络购物APP的banner很吸引人。此外，被采访者认为Shopee目前在搜索功能、产品信息列表、产品详细信息页、售后服务、退换服务等地方都做得不错。总之，他们在购买时和购买后的体验是比较不错的，而我们或许可以关注改善他们购买产品之前的用户体验。 **

Academic Research 学术文章调查

The Truth about Online Customers - KPMG 2017 Global Online Consumer Report

Suggestions for APP service: 该文章对移动APP服务有如下建议：

  1. 私人定制的服务很重要：服务商应该理解客户想做什么、买什么
  2. 在亚洲，品牌比价格更重要：用户在意价格，但品牌真的十分重要
  3. 让用户喜欢、信任你的网站很重要
  4. 信息安全已越来越重要

Lack of Real Data 缺乏真实数据

In fact, though we can draw many conclusion from current research, it is still unpersuasive to tell which issue is the most critical without the support of real data.

From my experience, real data can help designers detect the defective points more accurately. By checking page view, CTR (click-through-rate), exit rate, density map and so on, we can tell which part of the product hindered our users from purchasing a product more accurately. For example, statistics of page view and exit rate can tell us at which step most users jumped out of the purchase cycle. 尽管我们可以从目前的调研中总结出一些结论，但在没有更大的真实数据的支持下，以上结论的说服力仍有些欠缺。

从我的经验看，真实数据能帮助设计师们更准确地找到当下的设计缺陷。通过检查浏览量，点击率，退出率，热度图等数据，我们能够更准确地发现是哪些地方正在阻碍用户购买产品。例如，页面浏览量和退出率可以告诉我们用户通常会在哪一步跳出购买的链路。

Conclusion 结论

After understanding Shopee’s business objectives and listing all potential problems, I think many of them are pointing to one issue - we should focusing on attracting users to start their purchase on Shopee. Our research results have given many hints in solving this issue and I summarized what we can do in this redesign project. 在理解了Shopee的商业目标并理清了现存的一些问题之后，我认为它们中的许多都指向同一个问题 — 我们需要专注于吸引用户在Shopee上进入购买商品的流程。我们的调研结果已经在这方面给了许多提示，以下我将总结我们能在改版中修改的地方。

04 Summary 总结

In the final prototype, I redesigned the homepage layout and search box layout, hoping to catch new users interest and provide a more personalized shopping experience. There are many considerations for this results, including commercial development, strategic target, user experience, consumer psychology and so on.  在最终的设计稿里，我改版了Shopee首页和搜索栏的布局设计，希望借此吸引新的用户，并提供一个更定制化的购物体验。改版设计考虑到了诸多元素，譬如商业发展的要求，战略目标，用户体验，消费者心理等。

This is my first time to study deep into an E-commerce platform, especially the time is very limited. If I can have more time, I will be able to read more analysis reports, conduct more detailed research, create different wireframes and prototypes for testing and so on. As a result, I can understand the market, users, and product further more, creating a more pursuasive redesign proposal.
