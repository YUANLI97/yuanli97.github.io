Inspiration

I was inspired by an elder female friend who was passionate about restarting her career after staying at home taking care of her husband and son for more than 5 years. Then, I did some more detailed research and I found out that there are many Chinese housewives who wished to return to the workplace. Also, the society does need more labor force to meet the demand for economic development.



01 Background

Reflection

For one thing, middle-aged women in China have great desire to return to workplace. But they encountered many problems, including time conflict, lack of work experience and so on. It has been verified that women with children are less likely to find a job in today’s market.

For another thing, there are great demands of labor force in the market, regardless of the type of work. Moreover, companies present themselves in a way that middle-aged people find unattractive, or they set high standards such that it becomes more challenging for these people to get hired.

Thus, to satisfy the middle-aged users’ needs and also fill up vacant positions, it is meaningful to eliminate the gap between recruiters and unemployed middle-aged people.



02 Research



Reflection

Those middle-aged people tend to criticize positions based on the potential influence on their lives and families, and that’s why we should provide information about nearby facilities in our product. To provide efficiency and convenience, we should design tools such as filters to personalize their search experience. Besides, offline classes, workshops, visits and other offline events are needed to enhance their competitiveness. Interpersonal relationships and industrial news are also what they lack, and we may thus consider making a unified platform to share various career news and to socialize with industry pioneers. Although there have been similar products in the market, none of them have obtained comprehensive functions, and more essentially our product is aimed at people in middle ages.
